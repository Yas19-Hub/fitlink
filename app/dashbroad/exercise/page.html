Exercise Library

⚙️

Bench Press

A fundamental upper-body exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Bicep Curls

An isolation exercise for the biceps, typically performed with dumbbells or a barbell.

Squats

A compound exercise that works the thighs, hips, buttocks, quads, and hamstrings.

Deadlift

A compound exercise involving lifting a loaded barbell or bar off the floor to the hips.

Overhead Press

An upper-body strength exercise in which a weight is pressed straight upwards from the shoulders.

Pull-ups

An upper-body exercise where the body is pulled up with the hands.

Mountain Pose

A foundational standing pose that improves posture and strengthens the legs.

Downward Dog

An inversion pose that strengthens the arms and legs while stretching the spine.

Warrior Pose

A standing pose that builds strength and stability in the legs and core.

Tree Pose

A balancing pose that improves focus and strengthens the legs and core.

Child's Pose

A restorative pose that gently stretches the back and helps calm the mind.

Cobra Pose

A backbend that strengthens the spine and opens the chest and heart.
